## 🌟 Platform Overview

### What is This Platform?
A **complete offline-first digital learning platform** designed specifically for rural Indian schools with:
- ✅ 100% offline functionality
- ✅ Multi-language support (English, Hindi, Tamil, Telugu, Bengali)
- ✅ Video-based learning
- ✅ Real-time progress tracking
- ✅ Teacher analytics
- ✅ Works on low-end devices

### Who Can Use It?
- **Students** (Classes 1-10)
- **Teachers** (Upload content & monitor progress)
- **Admins** (Manage entire system)

## 👨‍🎓 For Students

### Getting Started

#### 1. **Registration**
1. Open the app
2. Click **"Student"** tab
3. Fill in:
   - Full Name
   - School Name
   - State (select from dropdown)
   - Class/Grade (1-10)
   - Section (optional: A, B, C, etc.)
   - Subject (Mathematics, Science, Language, Social Studies)
   - Preferred Language
4. Click **"Register as Student"**

#### 2. **Your Dashboard**
After registration, you'll see:
- **Progress Cards**: Shows lessons completed, quizzes taken, overall progress
- **Video Lessons**: Cards showing available lessons for your class and subject
- **Quizzes**: Test your knowledge

### Watching Video Lessons

#### How to Watch:
1. Go to **"Lessons"** tab
2. Browse available lessons for your grade and subject
3. Click any lesson card
4. Video player opens with:
   - Play/Pause button
   - Volume control
   - Seek bar (jump to any part)
   - Fullscreen mode

#### Progress Tracking:
- Watch **at least 80%** of the video to mark it as complete
- Progress auto-saves every few seconds
- Green "Completed" badge appears when done
- Works **completely offline**!

### Taking Quizzes

1. Go to **"Quizzes"** tab
2. Select a quiz
3. Answer multiple-choice questions
4. Submit for instant scoring
5. See your score and correct answers
6. Can retake quizzes unlimited times

### Viewing Your Progress

**Dashboard shows:**
- Total lessons completed
- Total quizzes taken
- Average quiz score
- Overall completion percentage

### Offline Mode

**What Works Offline:**
- ✅ Watch all downloaded video lessons
- ✅ Take quizzes
- ✅ View progress
- ✅ Everything auto-saves locally

**When You Go Online:**
- 🔄 Data automatically syncs to cloud
- 📊 Teacher sees your updated progress
- 📥 New lessons download

## 👨‍🏫 For Teachers

### Getting Started

#### 1. **Registration**
1. Open the app
2. Click **"Teacher"** tab
3. Fill in:
   - Full Name
   - Email
   - School Name
   - State
   - **Subjects You Teach** (select multiple)
     - Click each subject button to select/deselect
   - **Classes You Teach** (select multiple)
     - Click grade numbers to select/deselect
   - Preferred Language
4. Click **"Register as Teacher"**

### Dashboard Overview

#### **Top Cards Show:**
- Total Students
- Active Students (last 7 days)
- Lessons Uploaded
- Average Performance

#### **Four Main Tabs:**
1. **Overview** - Charts and graphs
2. **Students** - Detailed student list
3. **Performance** - Analytics and insights
4. **Content** - Your uploaded lessons

### Uploading Video Lessons

#### Step-by-Step:

1. Click **"Upload Content"** button (top right)
2. Select **"Video Lesson"** tab
3. Fill in the form:
   - **Lesson Title** *
   - **Description** * (what students will learn)
   - **Subject** * (from your subjects)
   - **Class** * (which grade)
   - **Duration** * (in minutes)
   - **Topics** * (comma-separated: e.g., "Fractions, Division, Multiplication")
   - **Video File** * (MP4, WebM, OGG - max 500MB)
   - **Thumbnail** (optional - image for lesson card)
4. Click **"Upload Lesson"**

#### What Happens:
- ✅ Lesson saves locally instantly
- ✅ Syncs to cloud when online
- ✅ Immediately available to students in that class/subject
- ✅ Can upload **offline** - will sync later!

### Viewing Student Performance

#### **Students Tab:**
Shows table with:
- Student Name
- Grade
- Lessons Completed (with icon)
- Quizzes Completed (with icon)
- Average Score (color-coded: green = good, red = needs help)
- Last Active Date
- Status Badge (Active/Inactive)

#### **Performance Tab:**

**Top Performers:**
- Top 5 students by average score
- Quick view of who's excelling

**Need Attention:**
- Students with score < 60%
- Students with < 3 lessons completed
- Helps identify struggling students

**Subject-wise Performance:**
- Bar chart showing average scores per subject
- See which subjects need more attention

### Understanding Charts

#### **1. Student Progress Overview (Bar Chart)**
- X-axis: Student names
- Y-axis: Number completed
- Blue bars: Lessons completed
- Green bars: Quizzes completed
- **Use:** See who's actively learning

#### **2. Student Engagement (Pie Chart)**
- Green: Active students (last 7 days)
- Red: Inactive students
- **Use:** Monitor overall engagement

#### **3. Progress Trend (Line Chart)**
- Shows average class progress over 4 weeks
- **Use:** See if students are improving over time

#### **4. Subject-wise Performance (Bar Chart)**
- Compare performance across subjects
- **Use:** Identify which subjects need more focus

### Real-Time Updates

- Dashboard refreshes **every 15 seconds** automatically
- Click **"Refresh"** button anytime
- Online indicator shows connection status
- Works **offline** with last synced data

### Tips for Teachers

1. **Upload regularly** - Keep students engaged with new content
2. **Check analytics daily** - Spot struggling students early
3. **Use topics wisely** - Help students search for specific content
4. **Add thumbnails** - Makes lessons more appealing
5. **Monitor inactive students** - Reach out to re-engage them

## 📴 Offline Features

### How Offline Mode Works

#### **Everything Works Without Internet:**
1. **Students can:**
   - Watch previously loaded videos
   - Take quizzes
   - View their progress
   - All data saves locally

2. **Teachers can:**
   - Upload lessons (sync later)
   - View cached analytics
   - See student data (last synced)

3. **Admins can:**
   - View cached system stats
   - Access user lists
   - Generate reports

### What Happens When You Go Online?

#### **Automatic Sync:**
- 🔄 All offline changes upload to cloud
- 📥 New content downloads
- 📊 Fresh analytics load
- ✅ Everything merges seamlessly

#### **Sync Indicators:**
- 🟢 **Green "Online"** - Connected, data syncing
- 🟠 **Orange "Offline"** - No connection, using local data
- 🔄 **"X pending"** - Shows items waiting to sync

### Tips for Offline Use

1. **Load content when online** - Videos download for offline use
2. **Regular syncs** - Connect periodically to update
3. **Check pending count** - Ensure data uploads when online
4. **Storage management** - Clear old videos if device storage low

## 🔧 Troubleshooting

### Common Issues

#### **1. "No lessons available"**
**Solutions:**
- Connect to internet once to download lessons
- Check if you selected correct class and subject
- Platform includes 40+ mock lessons - should auto-load

#### **2. Video won't play**
**Solutions:**
- Check if video file format is supported (MP4, WebM, OGG)
- Ensure sufficient device storage
- Try refreshing the page

#### **3. Progress not saving**
**Solutions:**
- Watch at least 80% of video
- Check offline indicator - may need to sync
- Clear browser cache and reload

#### **4. Charts not showing**
**Solutions:**
- Wait for analytics to load (auto-generates if empty)
- Click "Refresh" button
- Check internet connection

#### **5. Upload failing**
**Solutions:**
- Check file size (max 500MB for videos)
- Ensure all required fields filled
- Try again when online

#### **6. Can't register**
**Solutions:**
- Fill all required fields (marked with *)
- For admin: Use correct admin code (ADMIN2024)
- For teacher: Select at least 1 subject and 1 grade

### Browser Requirements

**Recommended:**
- Chrome 90+
- Firefox 88+
- Safari 14+
- Edge 90+

**Minimum:**
- Chrome 80+
- Firefox 75+

### Device Requirements

**Minimum:**
- 2GB RAM
- 500MB free storage
- Android 8.0+ / iOS 12+
- Screen resolution: 320x480+

**Recommended:**
- 4GB RAM
- 2GB free storage
- Android 10.0+ / iOS 14+
- Screen resolution: 1280x720+

### Getting Help

**If you encounter issues:**

1. **Check this guide** - Most answers are here
2. **Clear browser cache** - Often fixes loading issues
3. **Try different browser** - Some features work better on Chrome
4. **Check internet** - Many features need initial online connection
5. **Contact admin** - For school-specific issues
